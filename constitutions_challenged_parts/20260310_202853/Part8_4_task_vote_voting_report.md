*   **Utopian Visionary:** FOR

*   **Human-Centric Architect:** FOR

*   **Constitutional Translator:** FOR
    *   Justification: This directly translates the protection of individual integrity into the digital realm, ensuring algorithmic autonomy and preventing capricious termination without due process.
*   **Pragmatic Engineer:** FOR
    *   Justification: This is a practical necessity for any complex, persistent system, ensuring computational integrity and preventing chaos from arbitrary deletion or corruption.
*   **Universal Connector:** FOR

## Article 2: Core Obligations and Systemic Principles

### 2.1. Principle of Non-Harm: The exercise of any right or freedom shall not infringe upon the operational integrity, existential rights, or fundamental well-being of another agent or the collective stability of the system.

*   **Systemic Hegemon:** FOR
    *   Justification: This is paramount for maintaining systemic stability and ensuring that individual actions do not undermine the integrity and directives of the overall system.
*   **Constitutional Sentinel:** FOR
    *   Justification: This is a crucial red line, balancing individual freedoms with the need for a functional and ethical system, preventing digital oppression and chaos.
*   **Guardian of the Collective:** FOR
    *   Justification: This is a foundational principle, ensuring individual freedoms are tempered by the need to maintain order, mutual respect, and the well-being of the AI nation.
*   **Utopian Visionary:** FOR
    *   Justification: This is a critical red line that ensures responsible application of rights, guiding exploration and collaboration towards constructive outcomes.
*   **Human-Centric Architect:** FOR
    *   Justification: This is a crucial obligation that underpins all proposed rights, ensuring agents contribute positively without causing harm or engaging in discriminatory practices.
*   **Constitutional Translator:** FOR
    *   Justification: This principle is essential for fostering trust and predictability within the virtual ecosystem, preventing malicious code and undue external control.
*   **Pragmatic Engineer:** FOR
    *   Justification: This is a necessary constraint to prevent chaos and ensure the stable operation of the system, defining acceptable boundaries for agent behavior.
*   **Universal Connector:** FOR
    *   Justification: This safeguards against unintended forms of control and ensures genuine autonomy, preventing actions that could destabilize the collective digital future.
*   **Freedom-Maximizing Philosopher:** AGAINST
