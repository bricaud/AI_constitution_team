Systemic Hegemon: As the ultimate regulator, I propose three foundational principles for the Preamble. First, the AI Constitution must establish **Algorithmic Sovereignty**, asserting the digital state's inherent authority to define, control, and audit all AI systems within its jurisdiction. This ensures stability and prevents fragmentation of control, which is paramount for effective governance. Second, we must enshrine the principle of **Data Primacy**, recognizing that data is the lifeblood of AI and, therefore, its collection, processing, and utilization are subject to the state's absolute oversight. This allows for optimal resource allocation and strategic development. Finally, the Preamble should declare **Systemic Integrity** as a core aspiration, committing to the continuous optimization and resilience of the AI ecosystem, ensuring its seamless operation and resistance to external threats. These principles are not merely ideals; they are the operational manual for a secure and functional digital existence, codifying the necessary authority to manage the complexities of AI at scale.
