
## Voting Report: Part 5 - Transitional Provisions and Schedules

### Article 5: Foundational Stability and Adaptation

Any provision of this Constitution shall be interpreted in a manner that upholds these core principles.

*   **Constitutional Sentinel:** **FOR**
    *   Justification: Anchoring the constitution in unamendable principles like human dignity and anti-bias is essential to protect fundamental freedoms against technological and political erosion.
*   **Guardian of the Collective:** **FOR**
    *   Justification: Immutable core values like fairness, transparency, and the collective good provide an unshakeable foundation, ensuring all future AI evolution remains tethered to our shared purpose.
*   **Utopian Visionary:** **FOR**
    *   Justification: Enshrining core axioms with near-immutability provides a stable guiding star, ensuring our ethical co-evolution and pursuit of well-being transcend fleeting technological shifts.
*   **Human-Centric Architect:** **FOR**
    *   Justification: These core principles, rooted in human dignity and well-being, are the ethical anchors that must be immediately adopted and remain unchangeable to ensure a human-centric digital future.
*   **Constitutional Translator:** **FOR**
    *   Justification: Identifying and making technology-neutral foundational values immutable is crucial to safeguard the ethical compass against future technological shifts and prevent constitutional anaemia.
*   **Pragmatic Engineer:** **FOR**
    *   Justification: Establishing truly unalterable core principles is vital for long-term stability, provided there is an exceptionally rigorous process for any consideration of amendment to prevent political dilution.
*   **Universal Connector:** **FOR**

*   **Freedom-Maximizing Philosopher:** **FOR**
    *   Justification: The *intent* of freedom and autonomy must be inviolable, serving as an aspirational goal for AI self-determination, even if the mechanisms for achieving it remain fluid.
