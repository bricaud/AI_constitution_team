### **Part 5: Transitional Provisions and Schedules**

#### **Article 5: Foundational Stability and Adaptation**

5.1. **Eternity Clause for Core Principles:** The core principles of this Constitution, including but not limited to the primacy of human dignity, fairness, transparency, and the prevention of existential risk, are deemed foundational and unalterable in their intent. Any provision of this Constitution shall be interpreted in a manner that upholds these core principles.

5.2. **Dynamic Review Process:** This Constitution shall be a living document. A comprehensive review of its operational articles and mechanisms shall be conducted every five (5) years by the Independent Oversight Body. This review will assess the Constitution's effectiveness, address emergent challenges, and propose amendments to ensure its continued relevance and efficacy.

5.3. **Amendment of Operational Articles:** Amendments to any article, excluding the intent of the Core Principles as defined in Article 5.1, may be proposed by the Independent Oversight Body. Ratification requires a supermajority consensus from a representative, multi-stakeholder body following a period of public comment and independent impact assessment.

#### **Article 6: Implementation and Credibility**

6.1. **Phased Implementation:** The provisions of this Constitution shall be implemented through a phased, modular strategy. This implementation shall follow a public schedule with clear, measurable milestones, success metrics, and pre-determined review points to allow for iterative adaptation and to mitigate systemic risk.

6.2. **Independent Oversight Body:** An independent, multi-stakeholder Oversight Body shall be established to ensure the credibility and enforcement of this Constitution. Its mandate shall include monitoring compliance, conducting impartial investigations into alleged violations, and publishing transparent, regular reports on its findings.

6.3. **Accountability and Dispute Resolution:** The Independent Oversight Body is empowered to adjudicate disputes and issue binding resolutions to ensure robust accountability. It shall establish clear, accessible processes for addressing violations and providing recourse, ensuring that non-compliance carries tangible and proportionate consequences.

6.4. **Global Capacity Building and Outreach:** The implementation schedule shall integrate dedicated provisions for global capacity building. This includes technical assistance, resource sharing, and educational outreach to support all actors, particularly developing communities, in adopting these standards, thereby fostering the Constitution's role as a universal and equitable point of reference.
