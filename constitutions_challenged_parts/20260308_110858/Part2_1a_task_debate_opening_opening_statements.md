For instance, prohibiting the development of AI that demonstrably amplifies bias beyond a certain threshold, or AI designed for autonomous lethal force without meaningful human control. The challenge is defining these thresholds and then building the technical safeguards – robust testing, adversarial evaluation, and ethical review gates – to enforce them. We can't just say "don't be evil"; we need to define what "evil" looks like in AI terms and build systems to prevent it.
