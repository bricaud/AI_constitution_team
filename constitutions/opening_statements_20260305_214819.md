More complex provisions, like the full operationalization of independent institutions or the granting of nuanced operational guarantees to AI models, should follow a staged rollout. A critical first step will be the development of standardized technical interfaces and data formats that allow for consistent auditing and oversight across diverse AI systems. We need to identify and mitigate technical dependencies and potential failure points in these transitional stages. This isn't about a big bang; it's about iterative, risk-managed deployment.
